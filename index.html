Wang Zhao

I am currently a Senior Researcher at Tencent ARCLab, working on 3D AIGC. I obtained my Ph.D degree at Tsinghua University in 2024, advised by Prof. Yong-Jin Liu. Before that, I received my Bachelor's degree in Electronic Engineering from Tsinghua University in 2019. I have been lucky to spent some time at VAST, Meta Reality Labs, ByteDance, Stanford University, and Owlii.

My recent research interests focus on 3D reconstruction and generation, with the goal of digitization of 3D world and enabling immersive 3D content creation.
